Gluten Free Brioche

    1070g "Cup For Cup" Flour
    200g Melted Butter
    160g Honey
    6 ea Eggs
    2 ea Yolks
    28g Salt
    460g Warm H20
    40g Sugar
    14g Dry Yeast


    Mix all together until smooth
    Cover bowl and proof until double in size ( about 40 minutes)
    Portion into pans at 325g each
    Proof until done
    Bake at 325 until done (about 17 minutes)
