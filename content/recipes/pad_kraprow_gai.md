---
Title: "Pad Kraprow Gai"
---
<!--more-->

Mix sauce:
  - *1 tsp* Fish sauce
  - *1 tsp* Oyster sauce
  - *1 tbsp* Soy sauce
  - *1 tbsp* maple syrup

Saute:
  - Ground chicken in:
  - *2 tbsp* coconut oil

Add *couple tbsp* of **sauce**.

When cooked add:
  - Garlic
  - Habanero
  - Onion
  - Green pepper

Saute till dry

Add rest of sauce and deglaze.

When done remove from heat. Add lots of:
  - Thai Basil
