Title: "Pad Kraprow Gai"
---
Pad Kraprow Gai

Mix sauce:
Tsp Fish sauce
Tsp Oyster sauce
Tbsp Soy sauce
Tbsp maple syrup

Saute:
Ground chicken in
2 tbsp coconut oil

Add couple tbsp of sauce.

When cooked add:
Garlic
Habanero
Onion
Green pepper

Saute till dry

Add rest of sauce and deglaze. 

When done remove from heat. Add lots of:
Thai Basil
