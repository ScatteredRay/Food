Title: "Gluten Free Pizza Dough"
---
<!--more-->

Ingredients
------------

Mix:
  - *1 cp* brown rice flour
  - *1 cp* tapioca flour
  - *2 tsp* arrowroot flour
  - *3 tsp* xantham gum

seperate bowl mix:
  - *3/4 cp* warm water (between **110°** - **115°**)
  - *4 1/2 tsp* yeast
  - *2 tbsp* sugar

and proof

Mix yeast mix with:
  - *2 ea* egg whites
  - *2 tbsp* olive oil

combine and mix dry and wet, let rise.
